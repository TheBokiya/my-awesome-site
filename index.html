What does that logo mean?

Logos are very hard to conceptualize and design. I've always wanted to have a logo that has a strong sense of identity. I wanted a logo that represent me because that's what logos are for.

The three white pillars in the logo represents the three towers of Angkor Wat , also can be seen from the Cambodian flag . Angkor Wat has five towers but if you stand right in front of the temple, you can only see towers.
